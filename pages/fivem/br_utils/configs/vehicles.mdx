## disableTurbulence
Removes the turbulence that helicopters and plances normally experience while in air
```lua filename="config.lua" showLineNumbers
disableTurbulence = true
```
- disableTurbulence: `boolean`
  - If the features should be enabled


## disableAutoHelmet
Stops the player from wearing a random helmet after stading still on a bike or bycicle for a couple of seconds
```lua filename="config.lua" showLineNumbers
disableAutoHelmet = true
```
- disableAutoHelmet: `boolean`
  - If the features should be enabled
